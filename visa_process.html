Course Details

This comprehensive visa processing and documentation training program is designed to take you from beginner to professional level. You'll learn to handle various visa types (Tourist, Student, Work), manage documentation requirements, book embassy appointments, and provide expert visa consultation services. The course includes hands-on practical sessions, real-world case studies, and industry best practices.

Visa processing is a critical service in the travel and immigration industry. With increasing global mobility, the demand for skilled visa processing professionals continues to grow. This comprehensive training program covers all aspects of visa applications, documentation, and embassy procedures for major destinations worldwide.

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to Visa Processing

  • Understanding visa types and categories
  • Overview of major visa destinations (USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Schengen, etc.)
  • Visa application process overview
  • Role of visa consultants and travel agents
  • Legal and ethical considerations in visa processing

Module 2: Tourist Visa Processing

  • Tourist visa requirements for major destinations
  • Document preparation and verification
  • Financial documentation and bank statements
  • Travel itinerary and accommodation booking
  • Application form filling and submission
  • Common rejection reasons and how to avoid them

Module 3: Student Visa Processing

  • Student visa requirements and eligibility
  • University admission letters and acceptance documents
  • Financial proof and sponsorship letters
  • SOP (Statement of Purpose) and LOI (Letter of Intent) writing
  • Academic transcripts and certificates verification
  • Student visa application process for major study destinations

Module 4: Work Visa Processing

  • Work visa types and categories
  • Employment offer letters and contracts
  • Work permit applications
  • Professional qualifications and certifications
  • Work visa documentation requirements
  • Dependent visa processing

Module 5: Documentation & Embassy Procedures

  • Document verification and authentication
  • Translation and notarization requirements
  • Embassy appointment booking systems
  • Biometric and interview preparation
  • Application tracking and status checking
  • Post-submission follow-up and customer service

Note: The curriculum includes hands-on practical sessions, real-world case studies, document preparation exercises, and industry best practices to prepare you for a successful career in visa processing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need prior experience in visa processing to enroll in this course?

A: No prior experience is required. This course is designed to accommodate beginners. We start with visa processing fundamentals and gradually progress to advanced documentation and embassy procedures. However, having basic computer skills and an interest in travel/immigration will be beneficial.

Q: What tools and resources do I need for this course?

A: You'll need a computer (Windows, macOS, or Linux) with internet connection. We'll provide access to visa application forms, embassy websites, and documentation templates. No special software installation is required as training is conducted online through web-based platforms and practical exercises.

Q: Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

A: Yes, upon successful completion of the course, including all practical sessions and assessments, you will receive a certificate from GTAC, which is recognized in the travel and immigration industry.

Q: What is the payment structure and are there any discounts?

A: The course fee is BDT 17,000. You can pay in two installments (BDT 8,500 each) or get a discount of BDT 3,000 by paying the full amount (BDT 14,000) at the time of admission. The first installment is due at enrollment, and the second installment is due after the first month.

Q: What happens if I miss a class?

A: We understand that students may occasionally miss classes. We provide recorded sessions and supplementary materials. You can also schedule a one-on-one session with the instructor to catch up on missed content. However, regular attendance is highly recommended for the best learning experience.

Q: Will there be job placement assistance after the course?

A: While we don't guarantee job placement, we provide career guidance, resume building assistance, and connect students with our industry partners. Many of our graduates have successfully found positions in travel agencies, immigration consultancies, and visa processing centers. We also help you build practical experience during the course.
